About Ducks

Fun Facts

  • Are social
  • They have waterproof feathers
  • Like to dance
  • Bond through food
  • Their beaks are VERY sensitive

How to Aquire a Duck

  1. Find the duck
  2. Don't chase after it, introduce yourself
  3. Pick up the duck gently
  4. Put the duck down (Don't kidnap it)
